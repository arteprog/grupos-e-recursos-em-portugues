Daddy! explores the concept of an universal remote connected with an internet profile, thus incorporating any IR controlled device into a powerful home automation system with integrated actions and powerful triggers.

Daddy! is an ongoing discovery project, progressing on a two-week sprint pace. Visit group notes to be informed of our status.
